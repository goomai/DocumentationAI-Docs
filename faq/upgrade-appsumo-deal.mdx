If you're looking to upgrade, this guide will show you step-by-step how to upgrade to a higher tier.

**Thank you for purchasing our lifetime deal on AppSumo! 🎉**

<Callout uid="b7be033a-8d2b-4077-a335-00b86ad0728c" kind="note" collapsed="false">
  You can only upgrade to a higher tier during the duration of the AppSumo campaign.

  When upgrading to a higher tier, you only need to pay the difference between the two tiers.
</Callout>

## **Step-by-Step Guide to Activating your Lifetime Deal**

1. Click on your AppSumo profile dropdown arrow, and select '[**<u>Product</u>**](https://appsumo.com/account/products/)' from the list.

   <Image src="https://dxbnflddnlunfgvtqukc.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/public/documentation-images/org-96d3f74a-c743-4558-b57d-55ba958dcc0d/doc-b2985ceb-da68-4a84-b0e4-c87a4c80dece/1756140757015-7jkk8fimld3-appsumo-product.appsumo-product" width="1884" height="501" alt="" />

2. In the products page, click on the “**Select**” in **HumanizeAI&#xA0;**&#x61;ctions column .

3. Then, choose “**Change Plan**” from the dropdown list

4. Choose the plan that suits you best to finalize the upgrade and pay the difference amount to upgrade to the selected tier.

**🎉 Congratulations! You’re all set.**
